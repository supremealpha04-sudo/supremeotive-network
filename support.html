Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock a paid ebook? ➕
Click the "Unlock" button on any paid ebook. You'll be connected via WhatsApp to complete payment. Once approved, the ebook will be available in your library.
How can I donate/support the platform? ➕
You can support us through bank transfers (NGN, USD, GBP, EUR), cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH, USDT), or platforms like PayPal, Patreon, and Ko-fi. All details are in the "Support Our Mission" section above.
How long does unlock approval take? ➕
Typically 24-48 hours after payment confirmation. You'll receive a notification once approved.
Can I delete my account? ➕
Yes, go to your Profile settings and select "Delete Account". This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
