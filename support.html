Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock a paid ebook?➕
Click "Unlock" on any paid ebook. You'll be connected via WhatsApp to complete payment. Once approved, the ebook will be available in your library.
How do I create a support ticket?➕
Fill out the form above with your subject and message. Our support team will respond within 24 hours.
How can I donate/support?➕
Use bank transfers (NGN, USD), cryptocurrency (BTC, ETH), or PayPal/Patreon. Details in the Support Our Mission section.
How long does unlock approval take?➕
Typically 24-48 hours after payment confirmation. You'll receive a notification once approved.
